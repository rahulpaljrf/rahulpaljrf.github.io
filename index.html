Rahul Pal

Ph.D. Research Scholar (Mathematics)

Welcome! I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun. My research focuses on Graph-based Deep Learning, Bayesian Uncertainty Modeling, and AI-driven Healthcare Applications.

My work integrates mathematical modeling, topological data analysis, and deep learning techniques for medical image segmentation and predictive modeling using Electronic Health Records (EHR). For more details about my research contributions, please visit the Research and Publications sections.
